Bio:

Rev.Fr. Emmanuel Ezenwata (Holy Man) is a Catholic Priest of Awka Diocese and currently the Parish Priest of St.John the Apostle & Evangelist Catholic Church, Agulu, Anaocha L.G.A. of Anambra State, Nigeria, and initiator cum spiritual director of Angelic Adoration Prayer Ministry. Naturally, he is easy going, humble, simple, unassuming and lover of holiness of life. As a Firebrand Charismatic Priest, he dedicates his time to prayer and total well being of the faithful. Meanwhile, he uses Revivals, Conferences, Retreats and Crusades to reach out to the sick, the poor and souls thirsting for transformation of lives.
